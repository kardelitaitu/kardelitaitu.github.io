Welcome to oz-pos

This User Manual provides step-by-step guides for store managers, cashiers, and shop owners.

Managing Stores

Configure and add store outlets from your central administrator panel. You can define location-based metadata, warehouse assignments, inventory thresholds, and active terminals.

Cashier Shifts

At the start of every cashier shift, cashiers open a shift register session. The cash drawer amount is logged, and cashiers can execute checkouts. Every event is recorded in a secure, audit-friendly ledger.

Email Reports

oz-pos generates automated business intelligence summaries. You can receive daily, weekly, or monthly PDF digests directly in your mailbox showing total sales, tax calculations, top selling items, and cashier transaction audits.
